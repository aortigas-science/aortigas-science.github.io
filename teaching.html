Teaching

Teaching Experience & Philosophy

I am passionate about teaching and mentorship. My teaching philosophy centers on emphasizing scientific communication and encouraging students to pursue hands-on research experiences. I strive to create an inclusive learning environment where students feel empowered to engage deeply with the material and develop critical thinking skills that will serve them throughout their scientific careers.

Spring 2026

BIOL 230W - Molecules and Cells

Teaching Assistant | The Pennsylvania State University

Served as sole instructor for two student sections of 30 students each for the practical laboratory component of the course. Reworked many course materials to emphasize scientific communication and encourage students to pursue hands-on research experiences as part of their undergraduate program.

Successfully assisted several students to obtain positions in academic laboratories in the months after the course concluded, helping them transition from classroom learning to active research participation.
