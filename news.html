Recent News

2025

December 2025
Awarded the Peter J. Hudson Best Student Paper Award, recognizing the best student-led papers among candidates belonging to the Center of Infectious Disease Dynamics (CIDD).
December 2025
Our latest paper on high-fidelity long-read sequencing of an avian herpesvirus was published in PLOS Pathogens. This work reveals extensive intrapopulation diversity in tandem repeat regions. Read the paper
July 2025
Awarded the University of Michigan Postdoc Preview Award, selected from a highly competitive pool of applicants to participate in a fully funded two-day event to interview with prospective mentors for postdoctoral positions.
June 2025
Started mentoring summer undergraduate students through the Summer Research Opportunities Program (SROP). Currently mentoring Braylen Washington from the University of Florida on varicella-zoster virus genome sequencing.

2024

November 2024
Our paper on comparative analysis of multiple consensus genomes of the same strain of Marek's disease virus was published in Virus Evolution, revealing significant intrastrain variation. Read the paper
September 2024
Received the J. Ben and Helen D. Hill Memorial Fund Award recognizing outstanding graduate students in Plant Science and Genetics.
July 2024
Presented research on herpesvirus genomics at the American Society of Virology (ASV) Annual Meeting in Columbus, Ohio. The talk focused on high-fidelity long-read sequencing revealing extensive intrapopulation diversity in repetitive elements.
April 2024
Our review paper "Embracing Complexity: What Novel Sequencing Methods Are Teaching Us About Herpesvirus Genomic Diversity" was published in the Annual Review of Virology. Read the review

2023

September 2023
Successfully passed the comprehensive examination in the Bioinformatics and Genomics graduate program at Penn State. This milestone marks the transition to PhD candidacy.
October 2023
Represented the Bioinformatics and Genomics program at the SACNAS National Conference, promoting Penn State graduate programs to undergraduate students from diverse and underrepresented backgrounds.
July 2023
Presented at the 47th International Herpesvirus Workshop (IHW) in Montana with a talk on genomic analyses of Marek's disease virus, revealing variation within and between classic MDV strains.
